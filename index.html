FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do you handle all communications via email?

I prefer email communication because it allows me to provide thoughtful, detailed responses to each author I work with. Email creates a clear record of our discussions, ensures nothing gets lost or forgotten, and allows me to give your questions the attention they deserve without the pressure of immediate responses. This approach helps me maintain quality service for all my clients while respecting both your time and mine. You can expect responses within 24 to 48 hours during business days.

What types of books do you work with?

I work with a variety of fiction and non-fiction genres including romance, mystery, thriller, fantasy, science fiction, self-help, memoir, and literary fiction. I have experience promoting both traditionally published and self-published books. Each genre has its own audience and marketing considerations, and I tailor my approach accordingly. If you are unsure whether your book is a good fit, feel free to reach out and describe your project. I am happy to discuss whether my services align with your needs.

How long does a typical promotion campaign last?

Campaign duration varies depending on your goals and the type of promotion. A book launch campaign might run for 4 to 8 weeks, while ongoing visibility building is typically a longer commitment of several months. Building a genuine readership takes time and consistency. During our initial conversation, I will help you understand realistic timelines based on your specific situation and objectives. I believe in setting honest expectations rather than making promises that cannot be kept.

What can I realistically expect from book promotion?

Honest book promotion focuses on building visibility, connecting with potential readers, and creating sustainable growth over time. While I cannot guarantee specific sales numbers (no one honestly can), I can help you increase your online presence, build your author platform, coordinate reviews, and implement strategies that have worked for other authors. Success in book marketing is often gradual and depends on many factors including your book quality, genre, and target audience. I will always be straightforward about what is achievable.

Do you work with first-time authors?

Yes, I work with authors at all stages of their careers, including those publishing their first book. In fact, I find it rewarding to help debut authors navigate the promotion landscape. First-time authors often benefit from guidance on building their initial author platform, understanding the basics of book marketing, and setting realistic expectations. I will help you avoid common mistakes and focus your efforts where they are most likely to make a difference.
